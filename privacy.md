# Privacy Policy for LeanLine
**Last Updated: March 16, 2026**

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to LeanLine. Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using LeanLine, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 2. Data We Collect
LeanLine is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account with an email address, phone number, or real name. We collect the following minimum data to make the app function:

* **Anonymous Identifiers:** We generate a random, anonymous UUID to securely sync your data across devices and allow you to connect with friends.
* **Profile and Social Data:** You may optionally provide a nickname and an avatar photo. Please note that users you have accepted as friends will be able to see your nickname, avatar, and current streak days. If you actively engage in a challenge with a friend, they will additionally be able to see your relative challenge progress. Because LeanLine allows users to view this data, we cannot control the actions of other users (such as taking screenshots). We strongly recommend only accepting friend and challenge requests from people you trust.
* **Fitness Data (Zero-Knowledge Architecture):** Your absolute weight data never leaves your device. LeanLine stores your raw weight logs strictly locally and within your iOS Apple Health app. To power our social challenges, LeanLine uses a privacy-preserving calculation: our servers only ever receive and process your relative progress (e.g., "lost 2 lbs" or "lost 1.5%") to calculate leaderboard scores. We never upload, collect, or store your actual body weight on our cloud servers.
* **Siri & System Integrations:** LeanLine supports iOS system integrations (like Siri and Apple Shortcuts). Any health data submitted through these Apple services is protected and handled strictly according to this privacy policy.
* **Photos and Camera Access:** When you choose to upload a profile avatar or save a generated Share Studio card to your device, we will request access to your photo library. We only access the specific photo you actively select for your avatar, and we only write images to your library when you explicitly use the app's sharing or exporting features. We do not scan your photo library in the background, nor do we upload any unselected photos to our servers.
* **Device Information and Push Notifications:** To send you timely updates regarding friend requests, challenge nudges, and daily reminders, we collect your device's anonymous Push Notification Token (APNs) and basic device model/OS version. We do not collect invasive tracking identifiers such as IMEI, MAC addresses, or GPS location data. You can manage or disable push notifications at any time in your iOS Settings.

### 3. Optional Apple HealthKit Integration
As an optional feature, you may choose to connect LeanLine with Apple Health. LeanLine is fully functional without this connection. If you explicitly choose to enable this feature, LeanLine will request read and write access to your Apple Health data (specifically, Body Mass).

* If you grant permission, HealthKit data is used exclusively to import your past weight history into LeanLine and to seamlessly sync your new weight logs back to your device's Apple Health app.
* We do not use Apple HealthKit data for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial use cases.
* We do not sell, share, or transfer your Apple HealthKit data to any third parties.
* You can manage or revoke LeanLine's access to Apple Health at any time via your iOS Settings. Revoking access will not prevent you from using the core features of LeanLine.

### 4. Data Storage and Third-Party Services
We use Supabase, a secure backend-as-a-service, to store your anonymous profile, relative weight change records, and challenge data. Supabase complies with strict global security standards. We also use Apple for secure processing of in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not store or process your payment information.

### 5. User Rights and Data Deletion (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)
You own your data. You have the right to access, modify, or permanently delete your data at any time. You can instantly wipe all of your data, revoke your anonymous identifier, and delete your profile directly within the LeanLine app by navigating to Settings and selecting "Delete Account."
